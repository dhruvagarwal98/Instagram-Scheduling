As fashion becomes a more integral part of youth culture, it's important to understand its impact on the environment. Textile production, dyeing, and shipping all contribute to significant carbon emissions and water waste. Brands are now starting to implement sustainable practices to combat these issues, but consumer awareness and demand for eco-friendly fashion are crucial in reducing the industry's environmental impact.
