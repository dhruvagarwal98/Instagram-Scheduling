Used motor oil is a serious environmental hazard as it can seep into soil and groundwater. Once contaminated, it can be difficult and expensive to clean up. Studies reveal that a litre of oil can pollute up to 1 million litres of water, equivalent to 250 swimming pools. This highlights the importance of proper disposal of motor oil, which should be recycled or discarded at a designated oil recycling center.
