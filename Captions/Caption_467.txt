The acidification of seawater due to the accumulation of carbon dioxide is one of the biggest environmental threats facing our planet. The excessive release of CO2 by the oceans puts stress on marine life, disrupts ecosystems, and exacerbates the effects of climate change. This alarming fact emphasizes the urgent need to take action to reduce carbon emissions and protect our oceans.
