Text messaging has become an integral part of communication, especially amongst young adults. On December 3rd, 1992, Neil Papworth sent the first-ever SMS (short message service) to Richard Jarvis's Orbitel 901 phone with Vodafone's network. It simply said "Merry Christmas," and it marked the beginning of a new era in the history of mobile communication. Later on, text messaging evolved from being a basic feature to using emojis, gifs and chatbots.
