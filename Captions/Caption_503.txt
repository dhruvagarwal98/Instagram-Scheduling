The record was set by Indian kirtan singer, Girija Devi, at the age of 50, in 1985. She performed non-stop in Kolkata, India, for a total of 240 hours, breaking the previous record by a music band, Guinness Book of World Records. Devi's concert was attended by hundreds of people who stayed with her throughout her performance. She sustained herself with only water, milk, and honey during the 10-day marathon. Her incredible achievement showcases her dedication to music and spiritual practice, elevating her status as a renowned musician in India.
