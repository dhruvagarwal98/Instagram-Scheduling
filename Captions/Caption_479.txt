The Statue of Unity is a tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the first deputy prime minister of India who played a vital role in Indian freedom struggle against British colonialism. Constructed between 2013 and 2018, the statue is located on a river island called Sadhu Bet near the Sardar Sarovar Dam in Gujarat. The statue also features a museum and exhibition hall showcasing Patel's life, achievements, and role in India's independence.
