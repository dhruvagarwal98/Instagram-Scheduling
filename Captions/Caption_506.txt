Scientists at Johns Hopkins University studied the light emitted by over 200,000 galaxies and combined the colors to create an average, which resulted in a beige-tan color that covers the vast majority of the universe. This color is now referred to as "Cosmic Latte". This color is a result of the combination of light from all the stars, galaxies, and cosmic dust in the universe.
