The London Underground, commonly known as "the Tube", opened its first automated line, the Victoria line, in 1968. The system used new technology which eliminated the need for drivers and allowed for more frequent and reliable service. Today, the Tube is one of the largest and busiest metro systems in the world, with over 1.3 billion passenger journeys per year.
