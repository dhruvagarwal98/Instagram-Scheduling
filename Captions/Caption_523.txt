Discarded plastic waste has significant environmental impacts and can harm marine wildlife. Plastic breaks down into tiny microplastics and enter the food chain, affecting the entire ecosystem. Scientists estimate that by 2050, there will be more plastic than fish in the ocean. The majority of plastic waste comes from just five countries: China, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, and Thailand.
