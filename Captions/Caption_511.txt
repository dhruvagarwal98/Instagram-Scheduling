Built by Rick "Ormortis" Schreck in 2019, the massive board was created as an interactive installation for the Salem Historical Society's annual "Haunted Happenings" festival. The board spells out words with a planchette weighing over 400 pounds and boasts the eerie distinction of being recognized by Guinness World Records as the largest Ouija board in the world.
