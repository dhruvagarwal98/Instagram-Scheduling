La Paz is not only the highest administrative capital in the world but also one of the highest cities overall. Due to its high altitude, it experiences unique weather and climate patterns, which have influenced the culture and traditions of the city. The city has a rich history dating back to the pre-Columbian era and is now a vibrant and bustling metropolis with a mix of architectural styles and cultures.
