In Greek mythology, Pandora was created by the god Hephaestus as a punishment for Prometheus stealing fire from the gods and giving it to humans. She was given a jar by Zeus and told not to open it, but her curiosity got the best of her and she opened it, releasing all the evils of the world. However, contrary to popular belief, the jar was the actual container and not a box.
