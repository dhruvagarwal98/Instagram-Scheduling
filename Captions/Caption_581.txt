Harry Cafe in Tokyo allows customers to spend time with their resident hedgehogs for a fee. Visitors can cuddle and feed the hedgehogs while enjoying a cup of coffee or tea. The cafe also provides educational materials on the proper care of hedgehogs as pets. The popularity of these cafes has spread, expanding to other countries such as South Korea, Hong Kong, and the United Kingdom.
