The "Gangnam Style" music video became a viral sensation and cultural phenomenon due to its catchy tune, dance moves, and humorous lyrics. The video was so popular that it inspired numerous parodies and even political figures, such as Barack Obama, to imitate the dance. The music video has since become a symbol of South Korean popular culture and helped to expand the country's entertainment industry globally.
