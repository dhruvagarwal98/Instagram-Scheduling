Many people are familiar with the iconic pyramids of Egypt, but few know that the world's largest pyramid - based on volume - is actually located in Cholula, Mexico. Known as the Great Pyramid of Cholula, this structure was built between the 3rd century BCE and the 9th century CE. While it doesn't have the same shape or height as the pyramids in Egypt, it's still an impressive feat of engineering.
