On August 11, 2016, 2,016 people in China set the world record for the largest human mattress dominoes game. Participants were required to fall onto an inflatable mattress and knock over the next one. The previous record for this category was 1,200 people and was set in New Orleans in 2016. This record-breaking event was organized to promote mattress culture in China and showcased how creativity and teamwork can lead to something extraordinary.
